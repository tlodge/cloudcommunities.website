Communities in the Clouds is a project looking at how technology can better support high-density and high-rise residential communities. The tools available to tenants and owners are currently quite limited in spite of the many challenges and problems that come from high-rise living. This project will design,test and evaluate solutions to meet the specific needs of these communities.
